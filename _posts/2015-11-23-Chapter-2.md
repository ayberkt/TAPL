# Exercise 2.2.6

Let \\(a\\) be an arbitrarily chosen element of \\(S\\). We know that \\(a R' a\\) by the
definition of \\(R'\\). So \\(R'\\) contains elements \\((a, b) \in S^2\\) if and only if
\\(a R b\\) or \\(a = b\\) hence it is the reflexive closure of \\(R\\).
